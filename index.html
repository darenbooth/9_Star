9 Star Organization

Welcome to 9 Star Organization!

We are a peaceful Star Citizen organization that specializes in mining, salvage, and transportation. The only rule of the organization is NO GRIEFING! You may pirate other players for resources, but we DO NOT kill for sport. We are mostly casual players who enjoy the grind of the game and share a common goal of accumulating as much aUEC as we can get our hands on.

What can we offer?

We can offer you resources to use such as mining, salvage and transportation ships. We can offer our assistance with any other sort of help as well. (pickups, refuels, bodyguards, etc.) You can ask for anything you need.

How to Join?

If you are interested in joining, it is very easy. Just click on the RSI link below, click on your players icon in the top right, click account settings, click organizations, click search and join organization and search for "9Star".
